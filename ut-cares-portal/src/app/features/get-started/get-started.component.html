Provider Enrollment

Enroll your patient in Sonxtra Support™ quickly and securely. The process takes just a few minutes.

Before you begin, have the following ready:

  • person Prescriber Information — NPI, practice name, address, and contact details
  • people Patient Information — Name, date of birth, address, and insurance details
  • medication Prescription Details — Product, dosing, titration, and supply information

Enrollment Steps

  1. 1
    Prescriber Information
    Enter your NPI, specialty, practice, and contact information.
  2. 2
    Patient Information
    Enter the patient's demographics, address, and insurance.
  3. 3
    Product Selection
    Select Orenitram®, Remodulin®, or TYVASO® for this enrollment.
  4. 4
    Prescription Details
    Specify dosing, titration schedule, nursing education, and supply.
  5. 5
    Review & Attest
    Review all information, sign the attestation, and submit.
